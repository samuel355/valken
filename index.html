About Valken Logistics and Homes

We Build, Renovate and Deliver

Valken Logistics and Homes Limited is a real estate, construction and property management and a logistics firm incorporated under the laws of the Republic of Ghana, with it's main office in Kumasi - Patasi in the Ashanti Region of Ghana.

We pride ourselves on being a dynamic and innovative company that offers comprehensive solutions in both the real estate and logistics sectors. With years of experience and a dedicated team of experts, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for individuals and businesses seeking seamless, efficient, and reliable services.
